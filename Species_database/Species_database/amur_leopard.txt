Species: Amur Leopard

Scientific Name: Panthera pardus orientalis

Location:
Native to the forests of eastern Russia and northeastern China.

Population:
Estimated fewer than 120 individuals remain in the wild.

Threats:
- Habitat loss
- Poaching
- Climate change

Interesting Fact:
The Amur leopard is one of the rarest big cats in the world and can run up to 37 mph.
